Ring Sets

Certain rings can be worn together on a single hand to create a 'Ring Set'. Any such Set rewards the wearer with special bonuses to specific character statistics, defenses, or abilities.

Any character can wear up to two such Ring Sets at one time, one on each hand.

Ring Sets include:

Pirate











Composed of Shark Attack, Slash, Quicksand, and Keen Aye.
Provides a persistent Armor bonus.

Ninja











Composed of Shuriken, Mantis, Divinity, and Ghost.
Provides both an Accuracy and Dodge bonus.

Medic











Composed of Adrenaline, Bandage, Diagnose, and Defibrillate.
Provides a Footspeed boost.

Chef












Composed of Hack, Knife Sharpen, Pot Lid, and Meat.
Provides a Dodge bonus.

Space Trooper











Composed of Guns, Guns, Guns, Duct Tape, Improbability Sphere, and My Density.
Provides an Accuracy bonus.

Demon













Composed of Fire Rain, Scaredy Cat, Teflon Spray, and Iron Will.
Provides a bonus to your Health Regeneration rate.

Angel













Composed of Solar Rays, Hunter's Bow, Healing Halo, and Wish.
Provides a bonus to your Luck, which results in a greater chance of finding loot.

Prankster












Composed of Hot Foot, Heavy Water Balloon, Gumshoe, and Fleet Feet.
Provides a boost to your maximum Health.

Shaman













Composed of Dervish, Hornet Nest, Rock Armor, and Coyote Spirit.
Provides a boost to your Stamina Regeneration rate.

Athlete











Composed of Bump, Taunt, Turtle, and Fitness.
Provides a boost to your Debuff Resistance, reducing the power and effect of any Debuff used against you.
