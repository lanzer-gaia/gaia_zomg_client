Customer Service Contacts

If you experience issues during game play and need assistance, please refer to the issues listed below for the appropriate reports. Please note that your report will be handled in the order received.

Report Abuse

In the event that you experience any of the following issues listed below, there are two ways you can report.
- Adult content : Explicitly sexual or violent content (above PG-13 level).
- Abusive language : Content that promotes or provokes hatred towards an individual or group.
- Account or password scams : Asking for your password and/or threatening to do harm to you or your account.
- In-game hacking or cheating : Attempting to abuse or exploit the game system.

If you click the avatar that's bothering you, then click OPTIONS in the Targeting Info Box, and choose 'Report Abuse', you can file your report accordingly.

In the event that you cannot hover over the offending user’s avatar, click on the "Menu" button (found in the bottom right hand corner of the game screen) and then click the "Report Abuse" link to file a report immediately. In this case, you will need to provide the user’s name in the report.

Be sure to provide as much information as possible and only send one report per incident.

Reporting Harassment

If another user is abusing, harassing, stalking or continually bugging you, please fill out an "abuse and harassment" report so that the moderators can address the situation and prevent further harassment.
